# Revenue Optimization Simulation – MLB Ticket Pricing Strategy Overview

This project models ticket pricing strategy for a professional baseball organization using dynamic scenario simulation in Power BI.

The goal was to evaluate how pricing decisions interact with demand elasticity and team performance to impact total revenue.

## Business Question
How should ticket prices adjust under varying demand sensitivity and team performance conditions to maximize revenue?

## Methodology
* Modeled price elasticity to simulate attendance response

* Incorporated team performance demand shifts using What-If parameters

* Dynamically recalculated ticket, concession, and premium revenue

* Built scenario-based revenue sensitivity analysis using DAX

## Tools Used
* Power BI
  
* DAX (SUMX, SELECTEDVALUE, scenario modeling)
  
* Elasticity-based demand modeling
  
* Revenue sensitivity analysis

## Key Insights
* Revenue optimization depends on demand elasticity assumptions

* Strong team performance increases tolerance for pricing adjustments

* In weaker seasons, price increases amplify revenue downside risk

* Full revenue mix (tickets + concessions + premium) must be modeled together
